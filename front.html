Smart Public Transport Tracker

Real-Time Tracking & Management for Emerging Cities // SIH 2025

The Problem

Unpredictable Wait Times

Passengers in tier-2 and tier-3 cities face long, frustrating, and uncertain waits for public transport, disrupting their daily schedules.

Overcrowding & Safety

Inefficient vehicle distribution leads to severe overcrowding, posing safety risks and creating an uncomfortable travel experience for commuters.

Fuel Wastage & Pollution

Non-optimized routes, traffic congestion, and excessive idling result in significant fuel waste and contribute to urban air pollution.

Our Solution

Low-Cost IoT Kit

A plug-and-play hardware module (ESP32, GPS, GSM) installed on buses to transmit live location data, designed to work reliably even in low-bandwidth areas.

Real-Time Passenger Counting

An innovative ESP32-CAM integration provides live passenger density data, allowing for proactive crowd management and better vehicle deployment.

User & Admin Apps

A lightweight commuter app for real-time tracking and a powerful web dashboard for authorities to monitor the fleet and optimize routes with data-driven insights.

Intelligent Route Planning

Enter your destination, and our AI assistant will analyze real-time bus locations, routes, and passenger loads to suggest the most efficient way to get there.

Dynamic Fleet Summaries

With one click, generate an operational summary of the entire bus fleet. The AI identifies key insights, such as which routes are busiest or where buses are clustered.

Smart Transit Assistant
